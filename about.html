Who We Are

Ayansh Overseas Consultancy is a dedicated education consultancy helping students achieve their dreams of studying abroad. We guide them at every step—from selecting the right course and university to ensuring a smooth transition to their destination country.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accurate, reliable, and timely information so that every student can make confident decisions about their education and career abroad. We strive to be the bridge between ambitious students and top universities worldwide.

Our Team

Our team consists of experienced counselors, visa specialists, and travel experts who bring their expertise to ensure every student’s journey is successful. We believe in personalized guidance, focusing on each student’s unique aspirations and strengths.
